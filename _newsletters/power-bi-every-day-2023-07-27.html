{% raw %}PV (<rate>, <nper>, <pmt>[, <fv>[, <type>]]){% endraw %} Calculates the present value of a loan or an investment, based on a constant interest rate. You can use PV with either periodic, constant payments (such as a mortgage or other loan), and/or a future value that's your investment goal.
